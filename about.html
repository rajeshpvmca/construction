Company Insights

Since 1970

Started as a small family masonry firm, we have evolved through decades of hard work and dedication. We survived economic shifts by sticking to our core values: integrity and craftsmanship.

  • 1985: First Major Commercial Project
  • 1999: Expansion to International Markets
  • 2010: Green Building Certification

Consistent Expansion

Our growth is not just measured in revenue, but in the complexity of structures we build. Today, we handle multi-story skyscrapers and massive industrial factories with ease.

We have grown our workforce from 10 to over 500 expert engineers and craftsmen, ensuring consistent design quality across the country.
